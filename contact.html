Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Science Association?

Students from all departments are welcome to join. Simply fill out the membership form available at our office or contact us through email.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, there is a nominal annual membership fee that helps us organize events and activities. Please contact us for current fee structure.

Can I propose a research project?

Absolutely! We encourage students to propose innovative research projects. Submit your proposal to our research committee for review.
